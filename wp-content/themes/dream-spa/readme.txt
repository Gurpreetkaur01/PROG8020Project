Dream Spa Theme README.

Beautifully designed WordPress theme  that supports Custom Menu's , Primary Sidebar, Four Widgets area at the footer region,Post Meta etc. 
It has a pixel perfect design that's great for any beauty salon, health related websites who wants a new look for their site.

Install
-------
1. Download "Spasalon" parent theme from https://wordpress.org/themes/spasalon/
2. Go to your WordPress dashboard and select Appearance > Install, click Upload link.
3. Upload both Spasalon and DreamSpa Theme, then activate the DreamSpa Theme
